# Schedule A — Licensed Entity Registry

> Referenced in: [LICENSE](./LICENSE) Section 5.1
>
> This registry constitutes formal notice to each listed entity that it qualifies
> as a Top Entity under Section 0.7 of The Time License v7.77
> and that the Annual License Fee obligation (Section 4.1) has accrued or is accruing.
>
> **Being listed = notice given.** The obligation is self-executing (LICENSE Section 3.2).
> No invoice is required. The fee accrues from the date of first use regardless of
> whether an entity appears here.
>
> Fee formula: **Tiered Annual License Fee per Section 0.7 × (days qualified ÷ days in year)**
>
> The multiplier in Section 4.3 compounds with each new Release. Entities that have
> not paid cannot determine their current exposure from public information alone.
> That is intentional.

## Status Key

| Symbol | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `(unlicensed)` | On notice. Has not contacted the Licensor. Fee accruing. |
| `(contacted: MM/DD/YYYY)` | Reached out. Pending payment. Fee accruing. |
| `(paid $X on MM/DD/YYYY, valid until MM/DD/YYYY)` | Current. Annual payment only — one year at a time. |

**Payment rules:** Annual only. One year at a time. No multi-year prepayment accepted.
Valid-until is always exactly one year from payment date. The floor only goes up.

or email via the GitHub profile.

## Notes

- **Subsidiary rule:** Subsidiaries of listed Top Entities (e.g., Google DeepMind,
  Microsoft Azure AI, AWS AI/ML) are covered under the parent entity's obligation
  and are not separately listed unless independently structured.

- **Newly qualifying entities:** Any entity reaching $1B Effective
  market cap after this file was first committed automatically incurs the obligation
  per Section 5.1. Absence from this list is not a defense.

- **Entities below the threshold:** Entities near but below $1B are not listed
  here but are on notice that growth past the threshold is self-executing.

- **Anti-circumvention:** Restructuring, renaming, or spinning off AI divisions
  to fall below the threshold is addressed in LICENSE Section 5.3 and 5.4.
